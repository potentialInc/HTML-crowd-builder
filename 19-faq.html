Frequently Asked Questions

General

CrowdBuilder is a collaborative platform designed for entrepreneurs and innovators to share early-stage ideas, gather feedback, and build a community around their projects before launching.
Our platform is open to anyone over the age of 18 who is interested in entrepreneurship. Whether you are a seasoned founder, a student with a new idea, or someone looking to support new ventures, you are welcome here.
Yes, creating an account and browsing ideas is completely free. We also offer a free tier for submitting projects. Advanced features for project analytics may require a premium subscription in the future.

Account

Click the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of the homepage. You can register using your email address or connect quickly via Google or GitHub authentication.
If you've forgotten your password, go to the login page and click "Forgot Password". Enter the email address associated with your account, and we will send you a secure link to create a new one.
Yes. You can delete your account permanently from your Account Settings page. Please note that this action is irreversible and will remove all your posted projects and comments.

Ideas

Navigate to your dashboard and click "New Project." You will be guided through a simple form to describe your concept, target market, and current status.
Once submitted, your idea goes into the "Fresh" feed. Other users can view, comment, and vote on your idea. You will receive notifications when the community interacts with your post.
Yes, you can edit your project details at any time. However, if you make significant changes to the core concept, we recommend posting a "Pivot Update" in the comments to keep your followers informed.

Voting

Voting helps surface the most promising ideas. Users get a set number of votes per week to distribute among projects they believe in.
You cannot vote for your own projects. Vote manipulation (creating multiple accounts to upvote) is strictly prohibited and will result in an immediate ban.
