Project Story

Traditional air conditioning systems are inefficient and often run when no one is home, leading to massive energy waste. Our Smart AC Controller bridges the gap between old IR-controlled units and modern smart home ecosystems.

Unlike competitors, our device uses local AI processing to learn the thermal properties of a room, predicting exactly when to turn on to reach the desired temperature by the time the user arrives, without relying solely on cloud connectivity. It integrates seamlessly with HomeKit, Alexa, and Google Home via Matter protocol.

We plan to launch a Kickstarter campaign in Q3 2026 with a target price point of $49, significantly undercutting the current market leaders like Sensibo or Tado.
