EcoStream AI

Enterprise-grade energy optimization for data centers using predictive machine learning models to reduce carbon footprint by 40%.

1:45 Demo

The Story

Data centers currently consume about 3% of the world's electricity, a number expected to triple by 2030. While working as a systems architect at a major cloud provider, I noticed that cooling systems were running inefficiently, often based on static rules rather than real-time thermal dynamics.

EcoStream AI was born from a simple prototype script that monitored server loads and adjusted cooling flows dynamically. In early tests, we saw a 15% reduction in energy usage within the first week.

We are now building a plug-and-play API that integrates with existing BMS (Building Management Systems) to bring this efficiency to every data center, from on-premise server rooms to hyperscale facilities. Our goal is to save 1 Gigaton of CO2 emissions by 2030.
